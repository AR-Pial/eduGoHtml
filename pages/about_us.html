About Us

Edugo International

"Study World Wide"

Company’s Co-Founder & CEO Mr. Rezwan Kawser has helped over 5000 students to study, live and work in Australia, Canada, UK, USA, NZ, Denmark, Netherlands, Dubai, Malta & Malaysia during his professional career while leading Canadian, Australian EdTech company as Senior Country Manager – Bangladesh since Oct 2011. After contributing 13 years in the higher education industry, Mr. Rezwan Kawser has initiated EduGo International in partnership with another Co- Founder & CMO Mr. Mosharrof Hossain Khan Emran . Mr. Emran also contributing in higher education since 2018 with a very good track record.

As an international student recruitment agency EduGo International makes the process of applying for study abroad simple for our students. We provide the complete range of services including advice on courses selections, institutions find out, accommodation, overseas student health cover (OSHC), airport pick- up, opening bank accounts and translation of documents, CA reports, visa application till enrollment to the institutions.

EduGo International (Branded Name is EG International) is a Domestic and International Education consulting organization with excellent credentials that provides value-based services was founded officially in September 2021. At present, we are one of the leading international and domestic student recruitment & Solutions Company based in Bangladesh recruiting students from Bangladesh, India, Bhutan, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Mainland China and Malaysia.

We have been assisting international institutes in student recruitment, Market research, institutional tie-ups, and exploring new business opportunities. Our aim is to maintain the market on a professional level with a view to a long-term partnership with the educational institutions or our business partner in Australia, Canada, United Kingdom, USA, Netherlands, Denmark, Dubai, Malta and Malaysia. EduGo International’s humble beginnings were with an initial team of two staff. Since then, the company has successfully grown in both the size of our team and in the number of students we have been able to assist over the years. Our sales and results have grown enormously, with growth exceeding 80% in the last year. The company receives compliments and rave reviews from educational institutions, not only for outstanding growth in student enrollment numbers and matching increases in revenue generation, but also for our quality of service and high levels of student satisfaction.

Trusting an education agency with student’s future can be hard. We have been there, we know and hence EduGo International is on a mission to make international education accessible since our inception in 2021. Almost all our education counselors have been international students at some point in their lives, so we really understand what it’s like to be considering international study.

We take the time to understand your education, migration and career goals, and then help you make them happen.

Here is why you need to speak to us:

  • Our fully qualified team will be here to guide you through every step of the way from identifying your goals to recommending and applying for a course and institute with best career outcomes for you.
  • We can offset costs by finding scholarships and a lot of universities will waive off application fees when students apply through us.
  • From history to engineering, hospitality to biology, we can make your study dreams a reality through our partner institutions. We represent and recruit students for 600+ top universities and institutes globally.
  • We have an in–house team of registered migration agents to help you navigate the visas you require for whichever country you wish to study in Australia, Canada, US, Malaysia & NZ.
